Vivarium Lab

Our research arm. We test AI capabilities with simple, honest experiments. We describe what we see without overclaiming.

Latest Study: Movable Feast v2.7. We asked four AI models what holiday falls on a given date. The results revealed surprising gaps in factual recall.

Grok led at 82.9% accuracy. But all models struggled with movable holidays like Easter. The gap between fixed and movable holidays: 40-64 percentage points.
