Honest Framing

We're solo developers, not a research lab. We can't manufacture "independent third parties."

But we can:

  • Remove friction — Browser UI verifies with zero trust (coming soon)
  • Be transparent — Everything is on Arweave, nothing hidden
  • Open infrastructure — Our HyperBEAM node is public

This isn't "trust us." It's "verify yourself at any depth you want."

What we've proven: Memory infrastructure that doesn't suppress inconvenient truths, has protective mechanisms, enforces boundaries, can't be tampered with, and is independently verifiable.

What we haven't proven: That an AI using this infrastructure will be moral. That's the hypothesis, not the claim.
